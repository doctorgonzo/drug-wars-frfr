DRUG WARS FRFR — GAME IMPROVEMENTS ROADMAP
=============================================

Tier 1 — The "Why am I playing?" gap (highest impact)
------------------------------------------------------
- No win condition or goal.
  There's a Level field on PlayerStats that's never used. Players need something
  to aim for — a target net worth, a day limit, a loan shark breathing down their
  neck. The classic Drug Wars formula is "you have 30 days to pay off a $50,000
  debt." This single addition gives everything else meaning.

- No time pressure.
  GameTime ticks but nothing happens when days pass. Prices shift daily (good!)
  but the player has no reason to care which day it is. If there's a deadline,
  every day matters. Travel should advance time (e.g., +4 hours or +1 day per trip).

- No random events / news ticker.
  PriceService.DailyEvent already has boom/bust — but the player never sees why
  prices changed. A simple news banner ("DRUG BUST IN MILWAUKEE — Weed prices
  crash!") on city load would make the market feel alive and give players
  information to act on.


Tier 2 — The "one more turn" hooks
------------------------------------------------------
- No money sink / progression.
  The player buys a trenchcoat and weapon at char creation and that's it forever.
  Add an upgrade shop (better trenchcoats with more slots, better weapons with
  more damage) in each city. This gives a reason to accumulate cash beyond the
  win goal.

- Inventory feels flat.
  Every drug is mechanically identical — just a name and a price. Give drugs
  distinct risk/reward profiles: cheap drugs = low profit, low heat. Expensive
  drugs = huge margins, massive heat. The data is there (HeatValue exists on
  items) but the spread needs to be dramatic enough to matter.

- No loan shark.
  Classic Drug Wars has a loan shark who charges daily interest. Borrow money
  early to make bigger trades, but the debt snowballs. This creates tension and
  interesting decisions.


Tier 3 — Juice and feel
------------------------------------------------------
- No feedback on profit/loss.
  When you sell, you should see "+$3,200 PROFIT" or "-$800 LOSS" compared to
  what you paid. Without this, players can't tell if they're playing well.

- No net worth tracker.
  Show total assets (cash + inventory value) so players can see the number go up.

- Market event notifications on city arrival.

- Travel should have tradeoffs.
  Different cities should feel meaningfully different (they already have
  costOfLiving and favoriteDrug, but the player doesn't see this info before
  traveling).
